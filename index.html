Yumiri Hanamori is cast as Nibelcole in the anime Date A Live IV

Posted: May 20, 2022 | Published: Rafhael Lowix A. Homwad

On Thursday, Kadokawa announced that Yumiri Hanamori will play Nibelcole in the Date A Live IV anime. Nibelcole will make her first appearance in episode 9, which will air on May 27.

On April 8, the anime debuted on the AT-X channel. Crunchyroll is streaming the series as it airs, as well as an English dub. The anime was supposed to debut in October, but it was pushed back to this year for "various reasons."

Geek Toys' anime is directed by Jun Nakagawa (Date A Bullet, High School Fleet) (Date A Bullet, Plunderer). The series scripts are overseen by Fumihiko Shimo (Talentless Nana, Myriad Colors Phantom World), the characters are designed by Naoto Nakamura (High School Fleet, The Price of Smiles), and the music is composed by Go Sakabe, who returns from the previous three anime.

The opening theme song "OveR" is performed by Miyu Tomita, and the ending theme song "S.O.S." is performed by sweet ARMS. Tsunako illustrated the original light novel series, which Koushi Tachibana launched in March 2011. In March 2020, Kadokawa's Fantasia Bunko imprint released the 22nd and final volume. The novels will be published in English by Yen Press.
